AI Medical data classifier

This website allows users to incorporate convolutional neural networks into medical data classification and diagnosing. There are two available classifiers. The first one detects the type of brain tumor (pituitary, glioma, meningioma or healthy) based on provided brain MRI in the form of an image. The second is designed to identify abnormalities in phonocardiograms based on a .wav sound file.

Brain MRI classifier
Heartbeat classifier

Both classifiers were trained on publicly available datasets from kaggle.com , containing respectively 7100 and 3240 data points. The brain MRI model achieved an accuracy of 96% on the validation set, and the heartbeat model - 82%.
